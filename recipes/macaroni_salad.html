Classic Macaroni Salad

This macaroni salad always gets lots of compliments.

It's an easy recipe to make and has a pleasing taste that everyone seems to love!

Ingredients:

  • macaroni
  • mayonnaise and yellow mustard
  • sugar and vinegar
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • celery
  • onion
  • bell pepper
  • carrots and pimento peppers are optional

Steps:

  1. Cook the macaroni in salted water.
  2. Mix the mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, and seasonings together.
  3. Stir in the macaroni and remaining ingredients. Chill in the refrigerator.
